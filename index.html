Shurfing is now Spinlister

After two years of hard work, loads of fun and great international surf experiences we are proud to announce that Shurfing is merging with the American based company Spinlister. Spinlister is global sharing platform that rent and list bicycles, skis, snowboards, SUPs, and surfboards in over 60 countries worldwide. Spinlister insures surfboards and SUPs up to $2,000 for any theft or damage!

We would like to thank you all for being part of the Shurfing community the last two years and are confident that Spinlister will make the surfing sharing economy stronger than ever.

See it, Share it, Surf it!
